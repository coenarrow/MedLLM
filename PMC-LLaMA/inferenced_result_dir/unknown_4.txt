### Instruction:
In your capacity as a doctor, it is expected that you answer the medical questions relying on the patient's description.
Analyze the question and answer with the best option.

### Input:
###Question: A 3-month-old baby died suddenly at night while asleep. His mother noticed that he had died only after she awoke in the morning. No cause of death was determined based on the autopsy. Which of the following precautions could have prevented the death of the baby?
###Options:
A. Placing the infant in a supine position on a firm mattress while sleeping
B. Keeping the infant covered and maintaining a high room temperature
C. Application of a device to maintain the sleeping position
D. Avoiding pacifier use during sleep


### Response:

A. Placing the infant in a supine position on a firm mattress while sleeping


B. Keeping the infant covered and maintaining a high room temperature

C. Application of a device to maintain the sleeping position


D. Avoiding pacifier use during sleep


### Correct answer:

A. Placing the infant in a supine position on a firm mattress while sleeping


### Explanation:

The correct answer is A. Placing the infant in a supine position on a firm mattress while sleeping.

The baby was found dead in a supine position on a soft mattress. The mother noticed that he had died only after she awoke in the morning. No cause of death was determined based on the autopsy.

The mother should have placed the baby in a supine position on a firm mattress while sleeping.
